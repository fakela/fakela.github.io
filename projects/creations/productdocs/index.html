Product documentation, Information architecture,
Content strategy

Context

As Mindee’s first Technical Writer, the goal of this role is to help developers be successful by giving them access to clear and informative documentation.

Also, collaborating with the various teams in nurturing a healthy and welcoming community around the Mindee ecosystem.

Problem

Mindee API is being used by developers to automate critical processes such as expense management, accounting, AP automation, loan application, customer onboarding, and many more.

As part of this goal, they required a Technical Writer to:

  • Documenting aspects of the Mindee platform and API.
  • Come up with ideas for and write blog posts centered around topics like Mindee, computer vision, machine learning or anything of interest for developers.
  • Write technical tutorials about Mindee, our API and our SDKs.
  • Work closely with the teams.
  • Provide feedback on text-based content created by others and advocate for good documentation practices.

Contribution

I started with understanding the problem our customers usually face, by talking to various stakeholders including the CEO, CTO, Developer teams, Customer success team, sales team, Marketing team, and Product Manager

Over the course of the project, I:

  • Assisted with a content audit of the existing documentation.
  • Facilitated a tree-testing exercise to identify gaps in our current documentation.
  • Managed an information architecture project to improve our documentation structure.
    • Evolved the documentation to be more task based, and also incorporated interactivity with images in documentation
  • Wrote technical content for our product features.
  • Improved documentation experience across our products

Impact

  • Set up a content strategy to tackle various pieces of technical content around our products.
  • Delivered a range of improvements to the documentation experience by undertaking information architecture and design activities.
