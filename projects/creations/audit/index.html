Documentation Content Audit and Content Strategy

Context

Worked with DocumentWrite in conducting documentation content audit and improving documentation experience for various teams.

Problem

DocumentWrite provides documentation services to companies who are looking to improve their user experience, need a content strategy plan and architecture.

As part of this project, they required a Technical Writer to assist with reviewing and identifying loopholes in documentation and come up with actionable solutions.

Contribution

I started with understanding the problem they were trying to solve, by talking to various stakeholders including the Product Managers, Technical Writers and Developers across the multiple teams and product.

Over the course of the project, I:

  • Completed a content audit of the existing user documentation.
  • Conducted user interviews to get an in-depth knowledge of your users—their goals, perceptions, and experiences .
  • Created an content plan with mockups and content suggestions.
  • Implement content metrics and KPIs .
  • Created a comprehensive information architecture and documentation

Impact

  • Delivered technical content that helped partners understand how to get started and use the various of their products.
  • Set up a documentation content plan, metrics, process and workflow that was used by the partners in developing and improving their documentation.
