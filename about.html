About

Who I Am

I'm a technical writer with 6+ years of experience documenting APIs, blockchain protocols, and developer tools for companies like TON Foundation, Protocol Labs, Orbital, and Calimero.

Based in London. Currently taking a cloud computing course because I like understanding how things work, not just documenting them. I learn something new every day. That's the point.

How I Work

I work directly with engineering and product teams to understand systems from the inside. I sit in on sprint planning, walk through demos with developers, and test features before writing the documentation.

I use docs-as-code workflows with Git and Markdown to keep content synchronized and reviews fast. Linters like Vale and automated checks help catch issues early. I also set up CI/CD pipelines to keep documentation in sync with releases.

Depending on the team's setup, I work with platforms like Mintlify, Docusaurus, GitBook, ReadMe, Nextra, and Confluence.

I focus on what developers actually need: tested code samples, documented edge cases, clear navigation.
